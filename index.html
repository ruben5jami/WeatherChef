Mini Yogurt Popsicles


INGREDIENTS

2 Cups Greek Yogurt
0.5 Cup raw honey
1 Cup mixed berries, washed

HOW TO

Blend yogurt and honey together in a large bowl. Fold mixed berries in. Transfer into a container with a spout so the mixture can be into popsicle molds. Fill popsicle molds close the top and put in popsicle sticks.
